The **Population** workspace (`#/population/...`) is where care teams triage patients, hand work to AI agents, and close diagnosis gaps. It contains three lists in the left sub-nav.

## TOC Worklist

The TOC (Transitional Care) Worklist is the master patient roster for post-discharge follow-up. Patients are grouped by status:

- **Ongoing Call** — a live agent call is in progress.
- **In Queue** — queued for an agent to call.
- **Scheduled** — outreach scheduled for a future date and time.
- **Needs Attention** — flagged for human review (consent missing, identity unverified, escalation).
- **Enrolled** — successfully onboarded into a care program.

Use the toolbar to **search**, apply **multi-filter chips**, select rows for **bulk actions**, and paginate. Every row tracks TCPA consent, recording consent, and identity verification status.

## TOC Queue

The Queue (`#/population/queue`) is the prioritized work surface for AI agents. The KPI summary bar at the top shows queue depth, average wait, and SLA breach counts. Reorder patients in the queue from the **Call Queue** drawer in the agent table.

## HCC Worklist

The HCC Worklist (`#/population/hcc`) lists members with open ICD-10 diagnosis gaps for risk-adjustment coding. The table is sticky on both ends — the member cell pins left, actions pin right, and the middle 21 columns scroll horizontally.

### Columns

DOS, Open ICDs, Create Date, Chart Available, Support Team, Coder, Reviewers 1–3, Rendering Provider, Visit Type, RAF Score, RAF Impact, IPA, HP Code, PCP, Decile, Cohort, Risk Level, Advillness, Frailty.

### Open the diagnosis side-car

Select a row to open the **Diagnosis Gaps Details** side-car (582 px wide). It shows:

- The patient banner with avatar, name, sex, age, RAF score, and an impact pill.
- A segmented **HCC / ICD** view toggle.
- Toolbar actions: refresh, add ICD, verify, filter, sort, history, book, search.
- HCC cards grouping ICDs by hierarchical condition category. Each card shows the provider, RAF info, and an expandable list of ICD rows.
- **Overridden HCCs** and **Closed HCCs** collapsibles at the bottom.

Hover the **Open ICDs** badge in any row to preview that member's open codes in a 360 px popover without leaving the table.

## Filters and search

The Worklist toolbar's **search** input matches patient name, initials, or ID across the active list. Filter chips combine with AND semantics — clear them with **Clear All**.
