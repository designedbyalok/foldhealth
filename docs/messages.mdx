The **Messages** page is your secure inbox for SMS and in-app conversations with patients and care-team members.

## When to use it

- Reach a patient who prefers text over voice.
- Coordinate handoffs between care managers in a shared chat group.
- Route inbound messages by topic or business hours using rules configured in [Settings → Messages](/docs/settings/messages).

## Layout

The page is split into a conversation list on the left and the active chat thread on the right.

- **Chat list** — recent threads grouped by patient or chat group, with unread badges.
- **Chat area** — full message history with timestamps, delivery status, and attachment previews.
- **Composer** — type, attach files, or select a template; send with Enter.

## Chat groups

Care teams collaborate inside **chat groups**. Each group has its own routing rules, business hours, and assigned agents — configure these from the Messages settings panel.
