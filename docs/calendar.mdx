The **Calendar** page lets you manage appointments across your care team. It uses a day-week-month grid that follows the Fold Health theme (light, dark, blue, or plum) automatically.

## When to use it

- Block out availability and book patient appointments.
- Coordinate visits across multiple care managers from a single grid.
- Click an empty slot to schedule directly without opening a separate form.

## Schedule an appointment

1. Open the **Calendar** page.
2. Click an empty slot in the day or week view, or select **+ New** to open the schedule drawer cold.
3. Fill in the patient, date, time, duration, and notes.
4. Select **Save**. The appointment appears immediately on the grid and persists to your database.

## Theme

The Calendar respects the theme you choose in the [profile theme picker](/docs/themes). Switching to dark mode flips the grid surface, hover states, and past-day overlay without a reload.
