The **Calls** page (`#/calls`) is the operator surface for voice communication. It combines call history, an in-context patient profile, and a dial pad in one screen.

## When to use it

- Take or place a call without leaving Fold Health.
- Review past calls for a patient and replay recordings or transcripts.
- Dial an arbitrary number from the country-coded dial pad.

## Layout

The page has three regions.

### Top tabs

`All`, `Incoming`, `Outgoing`, `Missed`, `Calling Agents`. Each tab filters the left-panel history. Action buttons on the right cover keyboard shortcuts and Calls settings.

### Left panel — call history (350 px)

- **All Call Lines** dropdown — switch between phone lines you have access to.
- Search and refresh row.
- Scrollable list of calls with direction-coded avatars:
  - Outgoing, incoming, missed, answered, declined.
- A **call-back pin** keeps important numbers at the top.
- A **Dial a Number** block pinned at the bottom with a country-code selector and a dial-pad launcher.

### Main panel — active patient

- Patient banner with avatar, name, and `Patient · Sex · Age (DOB) · Phone` meta.
- Quick actions: Home, Call, Email, Chat, Video, Files, Call history.
- A 5-column table of recent calls: **Calls | Date & Time | Duration | Out of Office | Actions**.
- Per-row actions: play recording, view transcript, retry, more options.

## Place a call

1. Open the **Dial a Number** block at the bottom of the left panel.
2. Pick the country code from the selector.
3. Type the number, or open the dial-pad launcher.
4. Select **Call** to start the outbound call.

## Review a past call

1. Find the call in the left panel or in the main-panel table.
2. Select **Play** on the row to listen to the recording.
3. Select **Transcript** to read the AI-generated transcript.
4. Select **Retry** to redial the same number.
