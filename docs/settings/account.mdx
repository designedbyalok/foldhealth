The **Account** settings panel (`#/settings/account`) is your personal preferences hub. Update your profile, switch themes, and review the administrative role your organization assigned to you.

## When to use it

- Update your name, photo, or contact information.
- Switch theme to Light, Dark, System, Blue, or Astrana Plum.
- Review your administrative role and the practices you can access.

## Profile

Edit your display name, title, photo, and contact email. Changes save inline.

## Administrative role

Your administrative role is read-only here — it's set by an organization admin. The panel shows your role name and the practices and features your role grants access to.

## Theme

Open the [theme picker](/docs/themes) from your profile popover (top-right) to switch between Light, Dark, System, Blue, and Astrana Plum. System mode follows your operating system's color-scheme preference live.

## Preferences drawer

Open **Preferences** to update notification settings, language, and time zone. The avatar uploader supports drag-and-drop or click-to-browse.
