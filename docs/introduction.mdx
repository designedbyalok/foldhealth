Fold Health is a care coordination platform for Transitional Care Organizations (TCOs). It brings patient worklists, AI agent orchestration, population analytics, and program goal tracking into a single workspace for clinicians, care managers, and administrators.

## Core areas

The platform is organized around five workspaces you reach from the sidebar:

- **Population** — The patient worklist and agent queue. Patients are grouped by status (Ongoing Call, In Queue, Scheduled, Needs Attention, Enrolled) with search, multi-filter, bulk actions, and pagination.
- **Analytics** — Fifteen dashboard views with period selection, practice filtering, and drill-down. Use the Executive view for a high-level snapshot and switch to specialized views (Risk, Quality, SDOH, ROI Simulator) when you need depth.
- **Agent Builder** — A visual workflow designer for conversation nodes, conditional branching, and tool integrations. Preview agents in the chat panel and persist versions before deploying.
- **Settings → Agents** — Manage AI agents, goals, the knowledge base, escalation policies, and feature toggles.
- **Settings → Embedded Components** — Whitelist domains for iFrame embeds, configure embedded UI components (Prior Auth, Risk Dashboard, HEDIS Tracker), and review per-entity audit logs.

## Who this is for

- **Care managers and clinicians** running post-discharge follow-ups, chronic disease programs, and preventive outreach.
- **Operations and quality teams** monitoring population health, HEDIS measures, and shared-savings performance.
- **Administrators** configuring AI agents, escalation rules, embedded components, and access policies.
