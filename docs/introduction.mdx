Fold Health is a care coordination platform for Transitional Care Organizations (TCOs). It brings patient worklists, AI agent orchestration, population analytics, and program goal tracking into a single workspace for clinicians, care managers, and administrators.

## Core workspaces

The platform is organized around workspaces you reach from the sidebar:

- **[Home](/docs/home)** — Personalized dashboard with welcome banner, alerts, calendar, tasks, and quick notes.
- **[Population](/docs/population)** — The TOC Worklist, TOC Queue, and HCC Worklist. Patients are grouped by status with search, multi-filter, bulk actions, and pagination.
- **[Tasks](/docs/tasks)** — List or kanban view of every task assigned to you, your pool, or others — with drag-and-drop and functional grouping.
- **[Calls](/docs/calls)** — Take or place calls, review history with playback and transcripts, and dial members directly.
- **[Calendar](/docs/calendar)** — Day, week, and month view for appointments with theme-aware styling.
- **[Messages](/docs/messages)** — Secure SMS and in-app messaging with chat groups and routing rules.
- **[Analytics](/docs/analytics)** — Fifteen dashboard views with period selection, practice filtering, and drill-down.
- **[Agent Builder](/docs/agent-builder)** — A visual workflow designer for conversation nodes, conditional branching, and tool integrations.
- **[Campaigns](/docs/campaigns)** and the **[Email Builder](/docs/email-builder)** — Plan outreach campaigns and design the templates they send.
- **Settings** — [Agents](/docs/settings/agents), [Goals](/docs/settings/goals), [Embedded Components](/docs/settings/embedded-components), [Messages](/docs/settings/messages), [Account](/docs/settings/account), and more.

## Who this is for

- **Care managers and clinicians** running post-discharge follow-ups, chronic disease programs, and preventive outreach.
- **Operations and quality teams** monitoring population health, HEDIS measures, and shared-savings performance.
- **Administrators** configuring AI agents, escalation rules, embedded components, and access policies.

## Make it your own

Fold Health ships with five [themes](/docs/themes): Light, Dark, System, Blue, and Astrana Plum. Switch from your profile popover; the change applies instantly across every surface with a smooth cross-fade.
